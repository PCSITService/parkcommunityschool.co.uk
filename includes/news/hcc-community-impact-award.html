HCC Community Impact Award

We are delighted to announce that Park Community School were last night awarded Hampshire County Council’s ‘Community Impact Award’.

We have been successful in winning this award in respect of our MUNCH project, which aims to reduce food poverty for the benefit of local children, young people and families in Leigh Park.

We started MUNCH, which runs every day through the school holidays, Thursday tea times and the last Sunday of the month to ensure local families always have something to eat.

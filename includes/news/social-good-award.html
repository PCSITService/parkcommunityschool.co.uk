Park Wins Social Good Award

We’re delighted to announce our success in winning this year’s Social Good Award awarded by EDUcaterer at the Royal Garden Hotel in London on Tuesday 8 October 2019. Park Community School has been recognised for MUNCH, which ensures that no child, no matter what school or age need go hungry in the school holidays.

Winning an EDUcatering Excellence Award is a great achievement and one that is highly recognised across catering and education.

For more information about, or to donate to MUNCH please email MUNCH@pcs.hants.sch.uk

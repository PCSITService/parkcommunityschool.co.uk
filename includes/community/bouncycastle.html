Pirate Bouncy Castle Party Package

Description

This party takes place in the theatre and we supply a large inflatable pirate themed bouncy castle 6.8m x 5.7m x3.95m

Includes

  • 1 Pirate Themed Bouncy castle
  • We supply a variety of background music
  • Two tables that sit 24 children and covered with party table coverings
  • Hot water pots and water jugs available on request
  • Party host can bring their own food and it can be eaten in the theatre or if you prefer in the restaurant

Limitations

  • Children need to wear socks at all times and to remove glasses and jewellery for everyone’s safety
  • Bouncy castle can have 8 children at a time up to the height of 1.65metres
  • Maximum attending of 15 children
  • No pop corn or candy floss machines

Available Days & Times

Saturdays, Sundays and School Holidays

10:30 - 12:30 & 13:00 - 15:00

Two hours includes setting up and clearing away.

Optional Extras are Available.

Party Venue Space

Description

Wanting to create your own party, we can provide you the space you require. Prices vary depending on the size of room you have.

Venue Capacity Cost Notes
Lecture Theatre 20 guests (max) £69 for 3 hours. Guest capacity may be lowered depending on any equipment being brought.
Main Theatre 50 guests (max) £102 for 3 hours.
Main Theatre & Drama Studio 80 guests (max) £120 for 3 hours.

Includes

  • 3 Hour Space Hire
  • We can supply tables and chairs
  • We can supply a variety of background music
  • Party host can bring their own food and it can be eaten in either venue or in the restaurant
  • Hot water pots and water jugs available on request

Limitations

  • No candy floss or popcorn machines
  • If hiring a DJ or Entertainer – their public liability insurance of £5million needs to be provided

Available Days & Times

Weekday Evenings from 17:00

Weekends from 09:00 - 16:00

School Holidays from 09:00-17:00

Optional Extras are Available.
